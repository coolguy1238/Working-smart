Company

AI should work for everyone

ValueAI was built on a simple belief: that the most capable AI tools shouldn't be luxury goods. We're building AI that's genuinely helpful, honest, and free.

Our mission

The development of AI has largely proceeded on the assumption that capability and access are in tension — that the most powerful tools must be reserved for those who can pay premium prices. We reject that assumption entirely.

ValueAI exists to demonstrate that an AI company can be well-funded, technically excellent, and still choose to make its product free. We believe that when intelligent AI is accessible to students, researchers, small businesses, and individuals in every country, the aggregate benefit to humanity is far greater than any revenue we'd generate by paywalling features.

We're in this for the long haul — building AI that's safe, honest, capable, and open to everyone who needs it.

What we stand for

The principles behind every decision we make

01

Genuine helpfulness

We build AI that actually helps — not AI that hedges everything and adds disclaimers to every response.

02

Radical honesty

Our AI says what it believes, acknowledges uncertainty, and never pretends to know what it doesn't.

03

Universal access

No paywalls for core capability. If we build something useful, everyone gets it — not just premium subscribers.

04

Safety by design

We invest heavily in alignment research so our AI is trustworthy, not just capable.
