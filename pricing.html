Frequently asked

Is it actually free? What's the catch?+
Yes, it's actually free — no catch. ValueAI believes capable AI should be accessible to everyone, not gated behind subscriptions. We're funded to build an AI that genuinely serves people, not to extract maximum revenue per user.
Will it stay free, or will prices go up later?+
Our commitment is to keep core features free forever. We may introduce optional paid add-ons in the future for niche enterprise requirements, but the capabilities you're using today will remain free.
What's the difference between Personal and Pro if both are free?+
Pro includes 5× higher usage limits, priority access during peak times, advanced data analysis tools, and early access to new features. For most users, Personal is more than enough. Pro is ideal for heavy daily users or professionals who rely on ValueAI throughout their workday.
How is ValueAI different from Claude or ChatGPT?+
ValueAI offers comparable AI capability but makes everything free that competitors charge for. We're focused on removing barriers rather than maximising revenue.
Is my data private and secure?+
Yes. Your conversations are private and not used to train our models without explicit consent. Team and Enterprise plans include SOC 2 Type II compliance and additional data controls.
